FAQ

Common Questions

Is DecOne Eraser really free?

Yes, completely free with no limits. We don't charge per image, don't require an account, and have no hidden fees. The model runs entirely in your browser.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely. Your images never leave your device. All processing happens locally using WebAssembly and your GPU (WebGL). We have zero access to your files.

Why is the first image slow?

On your first use, the model (about 100–200MB) is downloaded from a CDN and cached in your browser. Subsequent images process instantly, and the model stays cached between sessions.

What file formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF. Files up to 15MB. Output is always transparent PNG (or WebP). For very large images, consider resizing first for faster processing.

How does batch processing work?

Switch to Batch Mode, add as many images as you like, then click "Process All". Each image is processed in sequence. When done, download all results as a single ZIP file.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! After the first use (when the model is downloaded), you can install DecOne Eraser as a PWA from your browser and use it fully offline. The service worker caches everything needed.
